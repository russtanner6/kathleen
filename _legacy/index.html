About

I am Kathleen Newrones

Kathleen is a Wholistic Wellness Healing Artist & Uplifter, weaving a golden tapestry of various art, wellness and healing modalities, including painting, photography, poetry, writing and sounds.

One of her specialties is expressing in her art the ancient healing language of the Soul, Source and the Cosmos through her Light Language. This evokes a celestial loving joy of remembrance of the truth of who we really are, a beautiful and magnificent sacred star.

She is an Ambassador for Mother Earth with a profound connection to the Ocean, the Sun and Spirit of Laguna Beach, for which she makes her home and draws her nourishment, replenishment, inspiration and joy.

She experiences Mother Earth Laguna, and of nature and life itself, as a symphonic thanksgiving and infinite blessing of divine grace, appreciating the love in and of everyone and everything.
